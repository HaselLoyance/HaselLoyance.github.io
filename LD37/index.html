technical name: LD37
real name: FeelsNeonMan
version: Release 1.0

One room full of deadly enemies.
You have a bunch of neat upgrades and big guns.
Can you survive long enough to fight the evil itself?

Theme: One room
Features:
-2D top-view neon-graphics shooter
-One room to have all of the action in
-Lots of weapon types/upgrades
-Amazing enemy mechanics
-Deadliest Final boss, that exists with you in one room
Controls:
A - Left
D - Right
W - Up
S - Down
Mouse left - Shoot
F2 - Game restart
CHEATS:
Although this game is pretty neat, it also is very challenging. If you find yourself stuck use keys O and P.
O - Lots of health and money
P - Go to the next wave
Game was done in 48 hours for Ludum Dare 37: http://ludumdare.com/compo/ludum-dare-37/?action=preview&uid=47228
